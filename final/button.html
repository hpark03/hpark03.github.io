FANTASTIC MUSHROOMS AND ALL ABOUT THEM
WHITE BUTTON
Where found:
White button mushrooms thrive in damp, fertile soil which is why they were originally found in horse manure! They are usually found in European and North American grasslands.
What you can eat it with:
These mushrooms are commonly eaten raw though they are also very frequently added as toppings for pasta, salads, soups, etc...
Fun Fact!
Button mushrooms are actually the same type of mushroom as portabella mushrooms! Portabella mushrooms are simply the adult version of button mushrooms!
