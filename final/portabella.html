FANTASTIC MUSHROOMS AND ALL ABOUT THEM
PORTABELLA
Where found:
These mushrooms are of Italian origin and get their name form the town of Portobello, Italy. These mushrooms are also very commonly cultivated in private gardens!
What you can eat it with:
In relation to their Italian origin, these mushrooms can be found accompanying a pasta dish or other types of sauces. Some eat them in burgers or even instead of buns!
Fun Fact!
Portabella mushrooms are highly nutritious and contain more potassium than a banana!
