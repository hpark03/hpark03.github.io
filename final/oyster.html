FANTASTIC MUSHROOMS AND ALL ABOUT THEM
OYSTER
Where found:
These mushrooms grow on logs and dead standing trees in subtropical or temperate areas! They can be found both in the United States as well as the United Kingdom.
What you can eat it with:
Oyster mushrooms can be eaten in a variety of ways, including whole, sliced, torn, or pan fried with any dish of your choice!
Fun Fact!
Oyster mushrooms are actually carnivorous. They feast on microscopic worms that are passing by and stunned by the powerful toxin that they release through their extended fibers!
