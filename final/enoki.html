FANTASTIC MUSHROOMS AND ALL ABOUT THEM
ENOKI
Where found:
These mushrooms originally were cultivated in Japan before they slowly started spreading through North America and East Asia. Now, you can find them in markets throughout Asia, North America, Europe, and Australia!
What you can eat it with:
Enoki mushrooms can be very quick and easy to cook up and eat! They are often cooked in hot pots, soups, stews, and noodle dishes like ramen and soba! They cannot be eaten raw though so make sure they are cooked thoroughly before consumption!
Fun Fact!
Enoki mushrooms get their name from the enoki, or Chinese hackberry, tree from which they grow in clumps of!
