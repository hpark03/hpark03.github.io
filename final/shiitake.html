FANTASTIC MUSHROOMS AND ALL ABOUT THEM
SHIITAKE
Where found:
Shiitake mushrooms were first cultivated in China a thousand years ago and are well known in Asia for both their culinary and medicinal qualities.
What you can eat it with:
Though increasing numbers of people have been eating this mushroom raw, it is highly recommended against due to possible severe skin reactions. They are usually added as an ingredient to various kinds of foods for their flavor or consumed as a side dish!
Fun Fact!
Though this is still being researched, studies have shown that shiitake mushrooms can improve well being, mood, and reduce fatigue!
